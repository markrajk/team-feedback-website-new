Giving feedback

The new simple way
to give feedback

Let your people give each other feedback with just a few clicks. Very easy, very quick and it’s make fun.

Learn more

Peer to peer feedback

One peer to peer feedback contains of three star ratings for the categories attitude, productivity and team working. In addition to that compliments or improvement ideas can be added. Giving feedback for example to five colleagues takes just around a minute. And by the way, it make fun.

Feedback routines

To increase the amount of given feedback team leaders can easily create feedback routines. For example that John will be requested to give Peter, Ella and Camille feedback twice a month. These feedback requests appear as a simple to-do list.

Short term and longterm development

The vertical bars show the average of the latest feedbacks, so basically how people are doing at the moment. The percentage value next to the bars describes the change. The horizontal bars describe the longterm average development.
