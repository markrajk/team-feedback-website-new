The dashboard

The dashboard to get all out of your new data

All basic features can be done with the app. In addition to that there is the dashboard for advanced analytics.

Learn more

Analyse the development of your teams

In the dashboard team leaders can easily analyze and compare how their teams are doing. For example in which teams people feel happy and productive and team work goes well and in which teams maybe not. And if not what could be the reason for it.

Analyse the development of your employees

Team leaders can easily follow how their people are doing. Who is doing well and who maybe needs some support. For example: What do Peter’s colleagues think about Peter at the moment? How do they see Peter’s productivity, how his attitude and team working? How does the trend of Peter’s development look like? What could be the reason for it?

Improvement ideas from team members

Team members have also the chance to give improvement ideas what we could develop in our team. These improvement ideas you will see by selecting a team and then click comments. A great new idea source for team leaders.

Summary

Let your people give each other feedback with just a few clicks. Very easy, quick and by the way it makes a lot of fun. In average Team Feedback sextuples the amount of written feedback in companies.
